Our Story

A Taste of
Tradition

✦

At Chianti Ristorante & Wine Bar, we don't just serve food — we bring people together over the joy of true Italian dining. For over 13 years, we've celebrated time-honoured recipes, crafted with fresh local ingredients and select Italian imports, to deliver bold, authentic flavours in every plate.

Step inside, and you're transported to a classic Italian trattoria — rustic charm, warm lighting, and the aroma of freshly baked bread creating a space made for memories.
